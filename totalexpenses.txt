While purchasing certain items, a discount of 10% is offered if the quantity purchased is more than 1000.
If the quantity and price per item are input,calculate total expense

INPUT:quantity: q,price per item: p
OUTPUT:Total expense: tot
1.Start
2.read no.of test case:t
3 for i from 0 to t:
     3.1 read quantity,price
     3.2 tot= q*p
     3.3 if q>100 then
            tot-=tot/10
     3.4 print tot
4.STOP
